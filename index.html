Meet the Team

I'm Michael Cunningham, the founder, lead editor, and FAA Part 107 Certified Remote Pilot behind Baseline Cinematic. I specialize in capturing premium 10-bit aerial footage and utilizing advanced node-based color grading in DaVinci Resolve to bring a unique, high-end cinematic look to the Peoria and Pekin area.

Baseline Cinematic is proud to be a husband-and-wife team. While I am navigating the skies and managing post-production, my wife expertly handles our stabilized ground cinematography. Utilizing advanced AI-assisted imaging, she ensures we capture dynamic, high-quality ground shots that seamlessly blend with our aerial operations to give you a complete visual package.
